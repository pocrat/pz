  • Mountain biking cowboys

  • Description

    A Cowboy themed event took place in a mountainous valley. Participants rode mountain bikes and showed off their biking skills while in cowboy outfits. After the trail ride, they hung out in the park to enjoy food, beer and conversation. The design places an emphasis on the use of western graphic and typography. I created posters, coupons, web banners &posts, and prize wrappers.
