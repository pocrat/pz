  • Spotlight app branding

  • Description

    Spotlight was an app first launched in early 2012 as Eventbase’s core product. It featured free event guides for the event organizers and attendees. My job was to create the Spotlight logo & other visual identity components, and incorporate them into the app’s UI. The composition, color and shape of the logo speak to the freedom, diversity, motion and energy.

    Spotlight app was replaced by Eventbase app which was named after the company itself in 2013. Surprisingly, the app is still alive on Blackberry’s app store. See link the section.
